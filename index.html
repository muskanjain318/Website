About Us

Hammerhead
Guide Service

Hammerhead Guide Service isn't just another fishing charter—it's a testament to Captain Jason Rice's lifelong passion for fishing. Growing up, Jason seized every opportunity to venture into the wilderness, honing his skills and deepening his love for the sport. Today, he extends this passion to anglers of all skill levels, from novices to experts.

Nehalem Bay, with its rich infusion of freshwater, is a haven for marine life, making it a prime spot for fishing enthusiasts. Whether you're keen on chasing Salmon, Steelhead, Sturgeon, or even indulging in some Crabbing, Captain Jason has got you covered.

Chase the Mighty Salmon

Dive into the world of Salmon fishing with Hammerhead Guide Service. Our charters, beginning in June or July, offer anglers the chance to catch pristine Chinook Salmon in the serene waters of Nehalem and Tillamook Bays. For those seeking a unique thrill, we also venture into the Pacific Ocean, targeting both Coho and Chinook Salmon. The ocean's vast expanse, when conditions are favorable, promises an unparalleled fishing experience. And for those who wish to combine their Salmon fishing adventure, we offer combined trips with bottom fishing and Nehalem Bay crabbing.

The Majestic Sturgeon Awaits

The waters of Nehalem Bay are home to the magnificent Sturgeon, a prized catch among anglers. With Captain Jason's expertise, you'll navigate the bay's depths, learning the art of Sturgeon fishing and experiencing the thrill of the catch. It's not just about the fishing; it's about understanding the habitat, the behavior, and the challenge that Sturgeon presents.

Discover the Ocean's Hidden Treasures

The ocean floor is teeming with life, and with Hammerhead Guide Service, you get to explore this underwater world. Targeting species like Lingcod, Rockfish, and more, our bottom fishing charters promise both excitement and a bountiful catch. With Captain Jason guiding the way, you'll learn the intricacies of bottom fishing, ensuring a rewarding experience.

At Hammerhead Guide Service, we guide you through the waters of Nehalem Bay, sharing techniques, tips, and insights to help you catch this prized fish. With Captain Jason's vast experience, you're in for an adventure that's both educational and exhilarating.

Dive into the World of Crabbing

Nehalem Bay's waters are rich with crabs, making it a hotspot for crabbing enthusiasts. Our crabbing charters are designed to offer a hands-on experience, from setting the traps to hauling in your catch. Perfect for families and groups, crabbing with Captain Jason is as much about the fun as it is about the catch.
